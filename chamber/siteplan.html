Site Purpose

This website is for businesses in the city of Orem. Its purpose is to provide a platform for business networking and fellowship opportunities, and to foster business growth.

Targe Market

Business owners and customers in the city of Orem and surrounding areas of UT

Site Goals

Networking opportunities for corporate PR to engage corporate members Encourage information for entrepreneurship and community development

User Personas

Small Business Owner: I am a small business owner who runs a Korean bakery at the age of 45. I am looking for franchisees. Startup: I am a 45-year-old Internet business owner who runs a gift shop. I am interested in helping with marketing support and sharing various business items and information. New Startup Supporter: Sharing information and business item support and sharing various information benefits.

Scenarios

Interested in building a network of local business owners and other business owners? Find out about membership, fees, and how to apply through the website. Set up events, workshops, and meet with various business owners.
