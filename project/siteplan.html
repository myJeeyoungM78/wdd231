Site Purpose

I'm planning a hobby-sharing and a small business website. The main page is designed to be an attractive advertising page with useful information about your hobby. It also has a child page where you can sell products related to that hobby.

Scenarios

Gift shoppers who want to give something special to special people. A gathering of people who want to make special gifts. A website where you can share ideas and find your own gift.
